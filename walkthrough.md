## Features Implemented
Here is what the final app features:

1. **Modern, Polished UI (Light & Dark Mode)**:
   - A perfectly responsive layout that cascades gracefully on mobile devices.
   - A toggle button in the header effortlessly switches between an ultra-clean Light Mode (`#ffffff` and light grays) and a sleek Dark Mode (`#0f172a` and slate grays).
   - Carefully tuned margins, paddings, border radii (`12px`), and soft layered drop-shadows provide a premium tactile feel.

2. **Motivational Header**:
   - The app dynamically injects a randomly selected motivational quote at the top of the app every time you load the page.

3. **Intelligent Form & Task Management**:
   - The "Add Task" area contains clear, spaced-out inputs for **Subject**, **Topic**, and the **Task Details**.
   - Input fields feature smooth `focus` micro-animations.
   - Tasks are rendered sequentially with custom-designed checkboxes.
   - Completing a task strikes through the text and changes its visual state.
   - Hovering over a task reveals a sleek `delete` button to remove it.

4. **Dynamic Progress Bar**:
   - Based on your completed vs. total tasks, a highly visible, fluid-animated progress bar fills up with a satisfying green (`#10b981`) color and updates the percentage in real-time.

5. **`localStorage` Persistence**:
    - Your daily tasks and dark mode preference are saved directly in your browser. Refreshing or closing the page won't cause you to lose your progress for the day!
